## 📱 SECTION 1: GRANITE COLLECTION (Hero Screen)
**Section ID**: `section-granite-hero`
**Component**: HeroCarousel.js

- Full-screen hero with rotating images
- Headline: "Global Suppliers of Premium Granite & Limestone"

- CTA buttons for browsing

- Hero text/headline
- Background images
- CTA button text
- Carousel speed

**Section ID**: `section-limestone-collection`

**What You Can Edit**:
- Product data

- Descriptions
